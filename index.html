Headstart School for Child's Total Development Inc. is a child-centered learning community in San Pablo City, founded on the belief that every child carries their own timetable for growth. We built a campus, curriculum, and daily rhythm around that belief — never the other way around.

Our Mission

To guide children toward becoming independent, disciplined, and compassionate individuals through a holistic, hands-on education.

Our Vision

A community of confident lifelong learners who lead with both intellect and character in a rapidly changing world.

What sets us apart: a genuine child-led teaching philosophy, delivered with the structure and rigor of a full academic track from Nursery through Grade 6 — under one roof, one community.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does enrollment open? +

Enrollment is open year-round for available slots, with priority scheduling ahead of the August school year opening.

How do I inquire about tuition? +

Tuition varies by level and payment plan. Message us directly or use the inquiry form below and our registrar will send full details.

What are the school hours? +

Classes generally run in the morning and early afternoon, Monday to Friday. Exact hours differ slightly by grade level.

Is a uniform required? +

Yes. Daily and PE uniforms are required and available through the school; sizing and fitting is arranged upon enrollment.

What grade levels do you offer? +

We offer Nursery 1, Nursery 2, and Kindergarten under Pre-Elementary, and Grades 1 through 6 under Elementary.
